Why AI Vibe?

Everything you need to redesign your space with precision and creativity.

LiDAR Scanning

Capture precise room dimensions and 3D geometry using Apple's LiDAR scanner in real-time.

AI Generation

Describe your dream room and get photorealistic design concepts powered by YandexGPT and GigaChat.

AR Preview

Place furniture and decor in your actual room with augmented reality before making any decisions.

USDZ Export

Export your room scans as USDZ files compatible with any AR-enabled device or 3D modeling software.

Privacy First

All processing happens on-device. Your room data never leaves your device without your explicit permission.

Modern Architecture

Built with Swift 6, TCA 1.19+, and Composable Architecture for a smooth, reactive, and testable experience.

How It Works

Three simple steps to transform your space.

1

Scan

Point your iPhone or iPad at the room and walk around. LiDAR captures every detail.

2

Generate

Describe your style — "modern loft", "scandi minimal" — AI creates photorealistic concepts.

3

Preview

Place virtual furniture in AR and walk around your redesigned room in real-time.

4

Export

Download your design as USDZ or share it with your architect or designer.
